📋 Ringkasan Metode Perhitungan
  • Quarter Mean Draft: (Fwd + 6×Mid + Aft) / 8
  • Trim: Aft - Forward (positive = trim by stern)
  • 1st Trim Correction: (Trim × LCF × TPC × 100) / LBP
  • 2nd Trim Correction: (Trim² × 50 × dMCT/dDraft) / LBP
  • Density Correction: Displacement × (Density Observed - 1.025) / 1.025
